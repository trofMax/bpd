About Us

Backpacker Deals is made up of people that love to travel. We're a close-knit team, made up of like-minded travellers and this team is the foundation from which everything else stems! We're passionate about giving everyone the ability to travel, and we love helping people's travel dreams come true. We're dedicated to finding the best tours and deals, and always strive to offer our customers a personalised booking experience.

When booking with Backpacker Deals, you can expect:

  • Great customer service - We understand how important it is to get your adventure right.
  • The best deals on tours and awesome activities
  • Our excellent relationships with tour operators means we can bring you memorable experiences at prices that won't blow your backpacker budget!
  • Travel knowledge - We can give you the best advice on where to go and what to do. Give us a call or take a read of our blog for useful travel tips.

Our belief is simple, to give everyone the opportunity to travel. Exploring the world doesn't need to cost the Earth. We aim to search the globe for the very best travel adventures at affordable prices. We make it easier for travellers to find exclusive deals through our website by offering hand-selected experiences we know they'll love. We ensure that all of our suppliers are passionate about offering unique experiences and pride ourselves in selecting small tour operators meaning we can give back to local communities. Everyone deserves the chance to travel.

Our credo

Our vision is to bring even more incredible experiences from the world over, directly to you! We reckon the world is a beautiful place and we want to bring you the whole thing. We have over 300 carefully-selected tours in 7 different countries, and we're constantly expanding. Life is too short to be wasted on going to work and paying bills. Whether it's skydiving in New Zealand, diving over the Great Barrier Reef or snorkelling with sharks in Fiji, we want everyone to experience travel with a wow factor!

There is a world of awesome adventures out there and we promise to find them for you.
So what are you waiting for?
